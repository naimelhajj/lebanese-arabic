<h2 class="wp-block-heading alignwide" id="books-faq">FAQ</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3,"align":"wide"} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading alignwide">Are these Lebanese Arabic books good for beginners?</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"wide"} -->
<p class="alignwide">Yes. Volume 1 is especially suitable for beginners because it starts with basic language and then moves into travel and everyday phrases.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3,"align":"wide"} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading alignwide">Do the books teach spoken Lebanese Arabic?</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"wide"} -->
<p class="alignwide">Yes. The focus is on practical spoken Lebanese Arabic rather than only formal or standard Arabic.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3,"align":"wide"} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading alignwide">Do the books include transliteration?</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"wide"} -->
<p class="alignwide">Yes. The books include Arabic script and Latin transliteration, which makes them more accessible for learners who are not yet comfortable reading Arabic script.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3,"align":"wide"} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading alignwide">Which volume should I buy first?</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"wide"} -->
<p class="alignwide">Most learners should start with Volume 1. Volume 2 works best as the next step.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3,"align":"wide"} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading alignwide">Are these books useful for travel to Lebanon?</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"wide"} -->
<p class="alignwide">Yes. Volume 1 includes practical categories such as airport, directions, money, accommodation, and shopping.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
