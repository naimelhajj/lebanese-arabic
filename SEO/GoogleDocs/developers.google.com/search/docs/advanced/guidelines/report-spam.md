# Report spam, phishing, or malware

If you find information in Google's search results that you believe
appears due to spam, paid links, malware, or other quality issues, use
one of the following forms. While Google does not use these reports to
take direct action against violations, these reports still play a
significant role in helping us understand how to improve our spam
detection systems that protect our search results.

## Spammy, deceptive, or low quality web pages

Ranking manipulation techniques that attempt to compromise the quality
of Google's search results violate our [spam policies](paid-links.html)
and can negatively impact a site's ranking.

<a href="https://search.google.com/search-console/report-spam"
class="button button-primary external-link">Report spam</a>

## Malware

If you believe a site is infected with [malware or distributing
malicious or unwanted
software](paid-links.html#malware-and-malicious-behaviors), notify us.

<a href="https://www.google.com/safebrowsing/report_badware/"
class="button button-primary external-link">Report malware</a>

## Phishing

If you believe you've encountered a page designed to look like another
page in an attempt to steal users' personal information, complete the
form below to report the page to the Google Safe Browsing team.

<a href="https://safebrowsing.google.com/safebrowsing/report_phish/"
class="button button-primary external-link">Report phishing</a>
