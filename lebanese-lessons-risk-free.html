Scheduling & How It Works

Your lesson is a reserved weekly timeslot that is dedicated to you. This ensures consistency for you and the teacher. If you need to reschedule a specific lesson, we require 24 hours' notice and will do our best to accommodate you that week, subject to teacher availability.

Our teachers are generally available from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM Beirut time (EEST/EET), Monday through Friday. When you fill out the form, please provide your availability in Beirut time to make scheduling easier.

Teachers & Method

Our first priority is to find a teacher whose schedule matches your availability. From there, we also consider your learning goals and current level to ensure the best possible match to help you succeed. All our teachers are native Lebanese speakers with extensive teaching experience.

Our method focuses on practical, conversational skills. We believe in learning through active use of the language. Lessons are structured but flexible, centered around real-world topics to get you speaking confidently as soon as possible. We provide all necessary learning materials, including notes and exercises after each session.
