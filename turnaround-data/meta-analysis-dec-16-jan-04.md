## 3. The "Founder" vs. "Social Proof" Disparity

**The Problem:**
Meta's algorithm has allocated **87% of the budget ($160)** to "Ad 1 - Founder", despite it performing significantly worse on every key efficiency metric compared to "Ad 2 - Social Proof".

*   **Cost Efficiency:** Ad 2 is acquiring customers **4.7x cheaper** ($17 vs $80).
*   **Click-Through Rate:** Ad 2 captures attention **2x better** (3.38% vs 1.71%).
*   **On-Page Quality:** Users from Ad 2 are **14% more likely to scroll** (54% vs 40%).

**Why is this happening?**
The "Founder" ad likely has more accumulated historical data (social proof, comments, likes) which the algorithm biases towards, even though its current marginal performance is decaying.

## 4. Action Plan

1.  **PAUSE "Ad 1 - Founder - Group Photo" (COMPLETED Jan 4).**
    *   **Reason:** Its CPP ($80.36) breached our RED LIGHT threshold ($80).
2.  **SCALE "Ad 2 - Social Proof - JD Video".**
    *   **Method:** With Ad 1 paused, the $10/day budget now flows to Ad 2.
    *   **Expectation:** If Ad 2 maintains its current efficiency, campaign-wide CPP should drop towards $20.
3.  **Monitor "Direct Action Video".**
    *   With Ad 1 paused, this creative might get some spend. Monitor its CTR. If it stays ~1.2%, it is likely a loser compared to Ad 2's 3.3%.

## 5. Capacity Constraint Note
While Ad 2 is highly efficient, remember that **Teacher Capacity is at 100%**. Scaling budget beyond $10/day is NOT recommended until we free up hours or hire. The goal now is **efficiency** (paying less for the same volume) rather than **volume** (getting more students we can't serve).
