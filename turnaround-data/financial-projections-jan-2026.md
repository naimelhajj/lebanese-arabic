# Financial Projections & Unit Economics (Jan 2026)
*Created: January 4, 2026*

This document details the financial modeling for the business's "Steady State" (current) and "Full Capacity" (target) scenarios. It serves as the basis for debt repayment and salary restoration planning.

---

## 1. Key Assumptions & Variables

### Revenue Assumptions
*   **Average Lesson Price:** **$21.50** (Based on $215 for a 10-lesson package).
*   **Weeks per Month:** **4.33**
*   **Realization Rate:** **90%** (Accounts for absences, pauses, rescheduling, and holidays where revenue is not realized).

### Cost Assumptions
*   **Transaction Fees:** ~4% of Gross Revenue (Stripe/PayPal + Currency Conversion).
*   **Tech Stack:** ~$50/month (Hosting, Email, Domain).
*   **Ad Spend:** $300/month (Maintenance Mode @ $10/day).

### Fixed Personnel Costs (Monthly)
*   **Teachers (Base):** ~$2,377 (Ella + Mireille + Ghina Base + Adon). *Based on Dec 2025 Actuals.*
*   **Admin Staff:** $400 (Natalee).

### Variable Personnel Costs
*   **Ghina (Overflow):** $11.00/hour (Paid only for lessons taught).

## 3. Scenario B: "Full Capacity" (Target State)
*61 Booked Hours/Week (+15 Hour Buffer Filled)*
*Buffer: 3 Hours (Mireille) + 12 Hours (Ghina Variable)*

### Incremental Revenue Calculation
*   **Theoretical Revenue:** 15 hours * 4.33 weeks * $21.50 = $1,396
*   **Realized Revenue (90%):** **$1,257**

### Incremental Cost Calculation
*   **Mireille (3 hours):** $0 (Already covered by fixed salary).
*   **Ghina (12 hours):**
    *   Calculation: 12 hours * 4.33 weeks * $11.00 * 90% realization
    *   Cost: **($514)**
*   **Transaction Fees (4%):** **($50)**

### Net Profit Projection (Full Capacity)
*   **Base Profit (Steady State):** $573
*   **Incremental Revenue:** +$1,257
*   **Incremental Costs:** ($564)
*   **TOTAL NET PROFIT:** **$1,266**

### Cash Flow Allocation
*   **Net Profit:** $1,266
*   **Eedama Debt Payment:** ($500)
*   **Available for Founder Salary:** **~$766**

## 4. Strategic Conclusion

*   **The Gap:** We are **15 billable hours** away from stabilizing the debt AND reinstating a partial founder salary.
*   **The Lever:** Filling the 12 variable hours with Ghina is highly profitable because the margin is **~49%** ($21.50 revenue vs $11.00 cost).
*   **The Risk:** Maintaining "Status Quo" leaves almost zero margin for error. One bad month or unexpected cost wipes out the ability to pay the debt. Growth is defensive necessity.
