# Growth Strategy: The "Private First" Pivot
*Created: January 4, 2026*

This document outlines why we are prioritizing "Private" and "Organic Pairs" over "Stranger Groups" and "New Curriculum" development for the immediate turnaround phase.

---

## 1. The Curriculum Trap (Why we are NOT building it yet)

**The Hypothesis:**
Building a structured curriculum/textbook/app will improve retention and allow for scalable group classes.

**The Reality (Jan 2026):**
*   **Conversion is High:** 67% Trial-to-Package rate proves product-market fit. Quality is not the bottleneck.
*   **Time-to-Cash is Long:** Curriculum work takes 50+ hours and pays $0 today.
*   **Unit Economics:** Selling a $20 book requires massive volume (150+ sales) to match the profit of just 5 private students.

**Verdict:**
**Shelved.** We will not invest unpaid hours into curriculum/product development until the $5,000 debt is cleared and founder salary is reinstated.

---

## 2. The Group Class Dilemma (Why "Stranger Groups" failed)

**Operational Failure Mode:**
*   **Cohort Churn:** Groups start full (5 students) but decay (2-3 remain).
*   **The Orphan Problem:** Surviving students cannot be easily merged with new students due to level mismatches.
*   **Scheduling Friction:** Coordinating 5 strangers' schedules is exponentially harder than coordinating 1 student.
*   **Volume Requirement:** To make "Level-Based Groups" work, we need 20-30 new students/month to backfill churning cohorts. We currently generate ~15.

**Verdict:**
**Abandoned.** We will not attempt to form cohorts of strangers. The operational fragility outweighs the theoretical margin.

---

## 3. The Winning Strategy: "Private Groups" (Lever 3)

**The Model:**
Incentivize leads to bring their own study buddies (friends, family, or colleagues).

**Why it Wins:**
1.  **Synced Retention:** Groups usually stay together or quit together. No "orphan" students to manage.
2.  **Zero Scheduling Friction:** We treat the group as a single unit.
3.  **Profit Multiplier:**
    *   **Private Hour:** ~$10.50 Profit
    *   **Group Hour (2+ people):** ~$35.00+ Profit (**3.3x+ Higher**)

**Execution:**
*   **Offer:** "Invite others to join for Free" (Trial).
*   **Upsell:** Discounted "Private Group" packages.
*   **Tactics:** Active on Landing Page (v2), New Year Email Sequence, and Confirmation Emails.

---

## 4. The Path Forward (Jan/Feb 2026)

**Focus:** **High-Ticket Boutique Private Tutoring.**
*   **Goal:** Fill the 15-hour operational buffer (3h Mireille + 12h Ghina).
*   **Method:**
    1.  Scale "Social Proof" Ad ($17 CPP).
    2.  Convert Solos and Pairs.
    3.  Ignore low-ticket products (Books/Apps).
    4.  Ignore complex operations (Stranger Groups).

**Success Metric:**
Reaching **$1,200/mo Net Profit** via simple, stable private/pair sales.
