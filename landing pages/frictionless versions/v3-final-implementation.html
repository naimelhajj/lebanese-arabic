Frequently Asked Questions

What is your teaching method?

Our method focuses on practical, conversational skills. We believe in learning through active use of the language. Lessons are structured but flexible, centered around real-world topics to get you speaking confidently as soon as possible. We provide all necessary learning materials, including notes and exercises after each session.

I'm a complete beginner. Is this for me?

Absolutely! Most of our students start from zero. We specialize in guiding absolute beginners through the fundamentals of Lebanese Arabic, from pronunciation to forming your first sentences. Our curriculum is designed for all levels, from absolute beginners to advanced speakers. No prior Arabic knowledge is required.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa and Mastercard cards, Link, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.
