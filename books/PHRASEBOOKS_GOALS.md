# Lebanese Arabic Phrasebooks: Project Goals

## 🎯 Primary Objective
The overarching goal of this project is to **migrate the Lebanese Arabic Phrasebook series from Amazon KDP to Bookvault** and establish direct-to-consumer (DTC) sales through our own WooCommerce website using Stripe.

## 💡 Strategic Purpose
1. **Higher Margins & Control:** Moving away from Amazon KDP's ecosystem to Bookvault's print-on-demand service allows for significantly better profit margins and total ownership of the customer relationship.
2. **Direct-to-Consumer Ecosystem:** Selling directly via WooCommerce enables us to capture valuable customer data, implement targeted email marketing, cross-sell our core tutoring services, and create high-value bundles (e.g., Vol 1 + Vol 2 + Audio).
3. **Quality Standardization:** Ensuring all physical products reflect the premium standard of the brand. This requires upgrading legacy assets (Volume 1) to match the professional, Adobe InDesign-based aesthetic of newer assets (Volume 2).

## 🚀 Key Deliverables
1. **Volume 1 Design Upgrade:** 
   - Replace legacy Arabic image glyphs with proper Right-to-Left (RTL) Arabic text.
   - Redesign internal tables to match the superior aesthetic of Volume 2.
   - Correct all known typos in the manuscript.
   - Complete the migration of the master file from MS Word to Adobe InDesign.
2. **Bookvault Infrastructure:** 
   - Prepare and export Bookvault-compliant print PDFs (interior and cover) for both Volume 1 and Volume 2.
   - Set up the titles, metadata, and print files within the Bookvault platform.
   - Approve physical proofs.
3. **WooCommerce Setup:** 
   - Design and launch high-converting product pages for Vol 1, Vol 2, and combined bundles on `lebanese-arabic.com`.
   - Integrate the Bookvault API/plugin for automated, hands-off print fulfillment.
   - Ensure Stripe payment processing is seamless, secure, and fully tested.
